Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

This site will contain content about my personal portfolio and all the assignments/projects I have done

Where will you get all the content for your site?

All content will be created by me. The text will come from my own experience, and project screenshots and code examples will come from my GitHub repositories and completed class assignments.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care because the site shows real programming work instead of just listing skills.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I want users to browse my projects, review my skills, and contact me if they are interested in working with me.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to showcase my abilities as a programmer and to help me gain opportunities in software development.
