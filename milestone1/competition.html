Competition

My main competition includes other student portfolio websites, GitHub profiles, and online coding portfolios such as personal developer websites.

What competitors do well

Many portfolio websites look professional, have organized project sections, and use modern layouts with screenshots and links to code.

What competitors do poorly

A lot of student portfolios are boring, poorly organized, or don’t explain what the projects actually do. Some only list skills without showing proof.

How I will do it better

My site will clearly explain each project, show screenshots, and link directly to working code. I will also keep the design simple and easy to navigate so visitors can quickly see my best work.
